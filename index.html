Lithium Hydroxide(LiOH), is a cathode material commonly used in Lithium-ion batteries like Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LiCoO3)

Lithium Hydroxide is an ionic compound consisting of Lithium, Hydrogen and Oxygen atoms.

Thus, it has a giant ionic lattice structure, and with strong electrostatic forces of attraction between oppositely charged ions,
it requires a lot of energy is needed to overcome forces of attraction causing it to have a high metling point
